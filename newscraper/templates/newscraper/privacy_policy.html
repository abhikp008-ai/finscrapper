1. Introduction

FinScrap ("we", "our", or "us") operates a financial news scraping and monitoring platform. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service that integrates with Google APIs.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Username and password for authentication
  • User permissions (monitoring and download capabilities)
  • Login timestamps and activity logs

2.2 Google API Data

Our application uses Google Sheets API and Google Drive API to:

  • Google Sheets API: Create, read, and write spreadsheet data for storing scraped financial news articles
  • Google Drive API: Manage file permissions and access to created spreadsheets

3. How We Use Google API Data

We use Google API access solely for the following purposes:

  • Data Storage: Store scraped financial news articles in Google Sheets as an alternative to local database storage
  • Data Retrieval: Read stored data from Google Sheets to display on the dashboard interface
  • Data Management: Organize and manage spreadsheet structure with proper headers and formatting
  • Export Functionality: Enable users to download CSV files of scraped data

4. Data Access and Sharing

4.1 Google Account Access

When you authenticate with Google OAuth, we request the following scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets - To create, read, and modify Google Sheets
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file - To manage files created by our application

4.2 Data Limitations

We do NOT:

  • Access any Google data outside of our application's created files
  • Read your personal files, emails, or other Google services data
  • Share your Google data with third parties
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Store your Google credentials - they are managed securely by Google's OAuth system

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Google Data Storage

  • Scraped news data is stored in Google Sheets owned by your Google account
  • You maintain full ownership and control of all spreadsheet data
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely and encrypted

5.2 Local Application Data

  • User account information is stored securely in our application database
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Access is restricted by user authentication and role-based permissions

6. Data Retention

  • Google Sheets Data: Retained in your Google Drive until you choose to delete it
  • Authentication Tokens: Stored until you revoke access or tokens expire
  • Application Logs: Retained for 30 days for debugging and security purposes

7. Your Rights and Controls

7.1 Google Account Control

  • You can revoke our app's access at any time through your Google Account Settings
  • You can delete any spreadsheets created by our application
  • You maintain full ownership of all data stored in Google Sheets

7.2 Application Account Control

  • Contact administrators to modify your user permissions
  • Request account deletion and data removal
  • Access and export your scraped data at any time

8. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with:

  • Google APIs: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Financial News Websites: We scrape publicly available news content from MoneyControl, LiveMint, and Financial Express

9. Compliance

This application complies with:

  • Google API Services User Data Policy
  • Google OAuth 2.0 Policies
  • Data minimization principles - we only request necessary permissions

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Information

For questions about this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact your system administrator.
